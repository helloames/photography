Hi! I'm Amy. I'm a Durham-based photographer and Duke student. Outside of photojournalism, I often shoot graduation photos for Duke seniors! For Summer 2025 and Spring 2026 inquiries, please reach me at amy.zhang@duke.edu. I open graduation photo session spots (for groups and individuals) once the previous academic semester ends.
