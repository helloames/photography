Hi! I'm Amy. I'm a Durham-based photographer and Duke student. I’m studying Computer Science with minors in Mathematics and Religion, and I’m currently the Photo Editor of The Chronicle, the student newspaper. I was previously the News Photo Editor, and I covered stories ranging from Governor Tim Walz and President Clinton on the Harris campaign trail to March Madness for Duke Men’s Basketball.

Outside of photojournalism, I often shoot graduation photos for Duke seniors! For Summer 2025 and Spring 2026 inquiries, please reach me at amy.zhang@duke.edu. Graduation photo session spots (for groups and individuals) are opened once the previous academic semester ends.

I’m also on the hunt for software engineering intern roles for Summer 2026. Past experiences include full-stack development projects sponsored by Apple, front-end software engineering at Duke's CFCI, and research on nuclear conflict modeling using machine learning. For Summer 2025, I'm interning at Fidelity as a software engineering intern.

Whether behind the camera or behind the keyboard, I’m drawn to building things that tell meaningful stories and make an impact. Feel free to reach out!
