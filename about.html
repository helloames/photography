Hello! I'm Amy

Hi, I'm Amy! I'm a Durham-based freelance photographer specializing in graduation, portrait, and engagement photography. My work blends candid emotion and polished professionalism, and I'm excited to work with you! My 2024-2025 bookings are open. If you'd like to contact me, please email me at amy.zhang@duke.edu. To see more of my work, check out my Instagram. To send tips to The Chronicle, go here.

Outside of freelancing, I'm a staff sports photographer and the Vol. 120 News Photo Editor at The Chronicle. I'm a Duke sophomore double-majoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I grew up in Charlotte, NC, and when I'm not on campus, I split my time between the beautiful Pacific Northwest and North Carolina. I enjoy spending time with family, hiking, and eating cheesecake.
