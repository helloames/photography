About Me

Hi, I'm Amy! I'm a Durham-based freelance photographer specializing in graduation and engagement photography and the News Photo Editor of Vol. 120 of The Chronicle. My 2024-2025 bookings are open. If you'd like to contact me, please email me at amy.zhang@duke.edu. To see more of my work, check out my Instagram. To send tips to The Chronicle, go here.

I grew up in Charlotte, NC, and when I'm not at Duke, I split my time between Washington and North Carolina. I'm a Duke sophomore double-majoring in Computer Science and Statistics.
